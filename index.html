Demand zones for cash. Demand and supply for F&O and indices.

The scanner now focuses on cleaner structure: order block location first, then liquidity sweep, then confirmation. RSI divergence is no longer used as a trade trigger.

Scan Logic

  • Cash universes show bullish demand-side setups only.
  • F&O stocks and indices can show both long and short setups.
  • `Sweep` means price took liquidity and closed back in favor of the setup.
  • `Confirm` means the latest candle followed through after the sweep/zone reaction.
