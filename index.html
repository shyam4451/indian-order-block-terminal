Indian Demand Zone Terminal

Demand zones for cash. Demand and supply for F&O and indices.

The scanner now focuses on fresh demand and supply zones built from swing structure and impulsive departures, then liquidity sweep, then confirmation. RSI divergence is no longer used as a trade trigger.

Scan Logic

  • Cash universes show bullish demand-zone setups only.
  • F&O stocks and indices can show both long and short setups.
  • `Sweep` means price took liquidity and closed back in favor of the setup.
  • `Confirm` means the latest candle followed through after the sweep/zone reaction.

How to use this version
